Welcome to ExtractMe

This website allows you to extract text from an image by selecting the specific region within the image that you wish to extract. Simply follow the steps below:

  1. Upload an image from your device by clicking the Browse button.
  2. Draw a rectangular region on the image that you wish to extract text from.
  3. Click the Extract Text button and the extracted text will be displayed in the text box below.
  4. Click on the Copy button to copy the extracted text to your clipboard.
  5. Repeat as many times as you wish!
